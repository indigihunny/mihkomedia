    Apex, Nunavut Zine

    Adobe Indesign | 2025

    The Apex, Nunavut zine is a passion project Kaitlyn created to showcase the skills she developed in the Digital Arts program at Selkirk College (Nelson, B.C.). Designed for print in InDesign, the zine features original visual assets created in Adobe Illustrator alongside photographs from her personal collection.

    Access Guide

    Adobe Indesign | Print | 2025

    In her role as mural festival coordinator, Kaitlyn collaborated with the Nelson District Arts Council and the Nelson International Mural Festival to design a 28-page accessibility guide for the Friday evening mural route, prioritizing a layout that was both family-friendly and easy to navigate. The guide was designed for both print and online distribution.

    Vector Illustration

    Adobe Illustrator | 2025

    For the 2025 Nelson International Mural Festival Block Party, Kaitlyn created a custom vector illustration for the staff and volunteer t-shirts.
